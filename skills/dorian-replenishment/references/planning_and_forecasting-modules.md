# Planning And Forecasting — Module Map

Planning and Forecasting module: combining historical data with promotional goals and plans to forecast sales velocity and inventory needs.

Two streams of inventory quantity data in NeonPanel: CALCULATED (from NP transactions) and 3PL (from external sources like Amazon Ledger).

FBA replenishment shipments and how NeonPanel calculates replenishment suggestions using Amazon Ledger data.
